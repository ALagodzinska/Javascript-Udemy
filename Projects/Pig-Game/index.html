GAME RULES

Player Number 1 starts the game. On each turn player roll a die, you can roll a die multiple times and the goal is to accumalate as many points as possible. Before each roll player can decide to hold collected points or continue rolling the dice. However, if player rolls a 1, then his turn is over and all accumulated scores for this round turns to zero. Player who first collected 100 points wins the game!
